Scheme Name: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) — Crop Insurance

What It Provides:
Comprehensive crop insurance coverage against losses due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases. Farmers pay a nominal premium — 2% for Kharif crops, 1.5% for Rabi crops, and 5% for commercial/horticultural crops. The remaining premium is subsidised by the central and state governments.

Eligibility:
All farmers, including sharecroppers and tenant farmers, growing notified crops in notified areas are eligible. Loanee farmers are automatically covered through their banks. Non-loanee farmers can enrol voluntarily through CSCs, bank branches, or the crop insurance portal.

Required Documents:
1. Aadhaar card
2. Bank account passbook
3. Land records (Khasra/Khatauni, land title, lease agreement for tenant farmers)
4. Sowing certificate from Patwari or village-level official
5. Crop details (area sown, crop type)

How to Apply:
1. Loanee farmers: Insurance is automatically deducted through crop loan accounts at banks.
2. Non-loanee farmers: Visit the nearest bank branch, CSC, or insurance company agent.
3. Alternatively, apply online at pmfby.gov.in.
4. Submit required documents and pay the farmer's share of premium.
5. Claims are settled based on crop cutting experiments and remote sensing data.

Where to Apply:
Banks, Common Service Centres, insurance company offices, or online at pmfby.gov.in.
