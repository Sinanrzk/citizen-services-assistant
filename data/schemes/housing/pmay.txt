Scheme Name: Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) — Housing for All

What It Provides:
Affordable housing for Economically Weaker Sections (EWS), Low Income Groups (LIG), and Middle Income Groups (MIG). Under PMAY-Urban, beneficiaries receive interest subsidy on home loans: 6.5% for EWS/LIG (loan up to Rs 6 lakh), 4% for MIG-I (loan up to Rs 9 lakh), and 3% for MIG-II (loan up to Rs 12 lakh). Under PMAY-Gramin (rural), financial assistance of Rs 1.20 lakh (plains) or Rs 1.30 lakh (hilly areas) is provided for house construction.

Eligibility:
EWS: Annual household income up to Rs 3 lakh. LIG: Rs 3-6 lakh. MIG-I: Rs 6-12 lakh. MIG-II: Rs 12-18 lakh. The beneficiary family must not own a pucca house anywhere in India. Women ownership or co-ownership is mandatory for EWS/LIG categories. For PMAY-Gramin, the household must be in the SECC permanent wait list.

Required Documents:
1. Aadhaar card of all family members
2. Income certificate or salary slips
3. Proof that the family does not own a pucca house (self-declaration affidavit)
4. Bank account details
5. Property documents (for Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme)
6. Caste certificate (if applicable for priority)

How to Apply:
1. PMAY-Urban: Apply online at pmaymis.gov.in or visit your nearest CSC.
2. PMAY-Gramin: The Gram Sabha identifies eligible beneficiaries from the SECC list.
3. Fill the application form with Aadhaar and income details.
4. For CLSS, apply through any scheduled bank or housing finance company.
5. Applications are verified by Urban Local Bodies (urban) or district authorities (rural).

Where to Apply:
PMAY-Urban: pmaymis.gov.in or Common Service Centres. PMAY-Gramin: Gram Panchayat or Block Development Office.
