Scheme Name: Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA)

What It Provides:
Guarantees 100 days of wage employment per financial year to every rural household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work. Wages are paid at the state-notified rate, typically Rs 200-350 per day depending on the state.

Eligibility:
Any adult member of a rural household willing to do unskilled manual work. The household must reside in a rural area and apply for a Job Card at the local Gram Panchayat. There is no income or caste restriction.

Required Documents:
1. Application for Job Card at Gram Panchayat
2. Passport-size photographs of adult household members
3. Proof of residence (ration card, Aadhaar, or any local address proof)
4. Aadhaar card (for wage payment via DBT)
5. Bank or post office account details

How to Apply:
1. Submit a written application or oral request at your Gram Panchayat for a Job Card.
2. Job Card is issued within 15 days after verification.
3. Once you hold a valid Job Card, apply for work in writing at the Gram Panchayat.
4. Employment must be provided within 15 days of application, or an unemployment allowance is payable.
5. Wages are credited to bank/post office accounts within 15 days of work completion.

Where to Apply:
Local Gram Panchayat office, Block Development Office, or online at nrega.nic.in.
